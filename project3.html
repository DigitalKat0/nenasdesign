How can individuals with limited culinary and organizational skills be motivated to cook more at home?

In this project, I built a meal keeper mobile app concept to assist students who are frustrated by their inability to save money because of excessive spending on dining out.

User Testemonies

After conducting qualitative research about potential user's eating habits, meal routines, and relationships with food, user stories were created.

Convenience

As a busy individual, I want convenient meal options that fit into my hectic schedule so that I can eat well without spending too much time on preparation.

Variety and Inspiration

As a meal planner, I want sources of inspiration to prevent monotony so that I can enjoy a diverse range of meals and avoid getting bored with my diet.

Cost Effectiveness

As a budget-conscious person, I want cost-effective meal solutions that align with my financial constraints so that I can minimize unnecessary spending on dining out and save money.

Efficiency

As someone who plans meals, I need guidance and support in making meal decisions, including assistance with grocery shopping, meal planning, and recipe selection, so that I can streamline the process and make efficient choices.

Ease of Cooking

As a person with limited kitchen space and cooking skills, I prefer simple and easy-to-prepare meal options so that I can cook meals without hassle and frustration.

Familiarity and Comfort

As an individual who enjoys routine, I appreciate familiar and comforting meal choices so that I can regularly eat meals that I know and enjoy.

Wireframes

A first iteration of wire frames were created. In the final wire frames, some details were changed based on feedback from users during interviews.

Personalization

When users have uploaded their recipes, the AI aspect will tailor the recipe's based on user preferences they have set. For example, if a meal is too costly, the ingredients can be replaced with a simpler and cheaper option. Or if the user would like to add meat to their meal a suggestion can be given.

How can AI be used in everyday tasks

I wanted to explore how AI can be helpful in recipe generation. It can be an easy tool to make quick substitutions and bring ease to a user who is new at cooking
