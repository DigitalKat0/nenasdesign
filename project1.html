BUDdy

A mixed reality app to help reduce exam stress

Overview

This app aims to design and develop a mixed reality application for students of all semesters in university. The app will help reduce exam related stress by encouraging and guiding students to take regular, restorative breaks during the exam period.

Existing Habits

Convince users to break out of their comfort zone.

Technical Components

One of our biggest challenges was integrating the two main technical components, as they were essential to shaping the overall user experience. We also had to ensure that users could trust the app, enabling them to build a consistent routine and structure around it

Define Target User

To prepare for interviews we wanted to precisely define our target audience, so contexts of use were created. Research on students gave feedback about their most common study spaces, tech habits, goals and motivations, and study behaviors.

Onboarding

Through a series of simple, illustrated instructions, users are guided through the app's core concept and main functions. The onboarding process proposes a way to create a study routine, choosing stress relieving activities, and having a personal growth chart through an AR plant.

Takeaways

Along with learning more in depth about the UX process, I also gained new experience managing a team and coworking with programmers.
